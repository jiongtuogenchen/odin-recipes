Best Italian Sausage Soup

Home

This soup was a big hit with guests. Just added toasted garlic bread and got effusive praise.

Ingredients

  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 2 ½ pounds pork shoulder roast
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar green salsa (such as Frontera)
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 serrano chile peppers, or to taste

Steps

  1. Layer onion into a slow cooker. Season pork with salt and black pepper; place on top onion. Pour salsa over pork; sprinkle in cilantro and serranos.
  2. Cover slow cooker. Cook on Low until pork is very tender, about 8 hours. Gently transfer pork to a cutting board. Strain and discard about 1/2 liquid from slow cooker; reserve remaining 1/2 liquid. Strain reserved liquid if desired, discarding onion, cilantro, and serranos.
  3. Shred pork using two forks; stir into reserved liquid, then serve.
